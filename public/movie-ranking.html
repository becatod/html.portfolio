The Best Movies According to Rebeca

My top 3 movies of all-times.

The Great Escape

A 1965 american movie inspired by real facts occurring in WW2.
Beside a great distribution, a lot of faimous actors are playing, the acting, scenary and directing are impressive.

Flags for our Fathers

Besides "Letters from Iowa Jima", these two movies are directed by Clint Eastwood, are about the desasters of any war on people, regardless of nation, religion or race.

The Idiot

A russian movie based on the book with the same name by M. F. Dostoievsky.
The acting is spectacular, the main character is fabulous, as are the other actors. A must-see of all times.
